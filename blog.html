Our Story

Why we built GigDial and how we're changing lives

Why We Built GigDial

In 2024, we noticed a big problem in India's gig economy. Workers struggled to find consistent work, while customers couldn't find reliable services. The existing platforms were either too expensive or didn't care about workers' welfare.

We asked ourselves: What if there was a platform that truly cared about both workers and customers?

"Ek Kaam, Ek Time, Ek Paisa"

One Job, One Time, One Fair Price - Our Promise to India

Our Mission

Empower India's 23.5 million gig workers with dignity, fair pay, and growth opportunities. We believe every worker deserves:

  • Stable Income: Through guaranteed leads and no-lead refund policy
  • Health & Wellness: Free health insurance for workers and their families
  • Skill Development: Free training programs and certifications
  • Fair Treatment: Transparent pricing and direct negotiation

What Makes Us Unique

100% Verified Workers

Every worker undergoes Aadhaar and PAN verification plus background checks. Your safety is our priority.

Direct Negotiation

Call workers directly and negotiate prices. No platform markup, no hidden costs. Fair for everyone.

Worker Wellness

Free health insurance, skill training, and mental health support for all verified workers.

Eco-Friendly

Filter for workers who use sustainable materials and eco-friendly practices.

Voice Search

Search in Hindi or English using voice. Perfect for low-literacy users.

AR Preview

Visualize painting colors or furniture placement in 3D before booking.
