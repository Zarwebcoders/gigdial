No-Lead Refund Policy

At GigDial, we guarantee that workers receive value for their subscription. If you don't get enough leads, we refund your money. No questions asked.

Our Guarantee

If you receive fewer than 10 leads per month, you get a 100% refund of your subscription fee (₹350).

How It Works

  • Subscribe: Pay ₹350/month for premium worker subscription
  • Track Leads: We automatically track all leads sent to you
  • Minimum Guarantee: You must receive at least 10 quality leads per month
  • Auto Refund: If you receive less than 10 leads, refund is processed automatically
  • No Waiting: Refund is credited to your account within 3 business days

What Counts as a Lead?

  • Customer views your profile
  • Customer calls you for a quote
  • Customer sends you a message
  • Customer requests your services

Important Notes

Leads must be genuine customer inquiries. Spam or fake leads don't count. Workers must maintain a 4.0+ rating to be eligible for refunds.

Worker Verification Process

Every worker on GigDial undergoes a strict verification process to ensure customer safety and service quality.

Verification Steps

  • Step 1: Document Submission - Upload Aadhaar card, PAN card (optional), and profile photo
  • Step 2: Identity Verification - We verify your Aadhaar and PAN using DigiVerifier/OnGrid (₹20-300 per check)
  • Step 3: Background Check - Criminal record check and address verification
  • Step 4: Skills Assessment - Optional skill test for certain categories
  • Step 5: Approval - Verification usually takes 24-48 hours

Verified Badge

Once verified, you receive a "Verified Worker" badge on your profile, which helps you stand out and get more leads.

Customer Benefits & Protection

We prioritize customer safety and satisfaction. Here's what you get when you use GigDial:

Safety & Security

  • 100% Verified Workers: Every worker is background-checked and Aadhaar-verified
  • Secure Payments: All payments through Razorpay with escrow protection
  • Privacy Protection: Virtual phone numbers via Twilio (₹0.75/min) protect your privacy
  • Dispute Resolution: 24/7 customer support for any issues

Quality Guarantee

If you're not satisfied with the service, we offer a dispute resolution process with escrow protection. Your money is safe until the job is done.

Dispute Resolution Policy

We handle disputes fairly and quickly. Our escrow system protects both customers and workers.

Dispute Process

  • Step 1: Customer or worker raises a dispute
  • Step 2: Both parties submit evidence (photos, messages, etc.)
  • Step 3: GigDial team reviews the case (24-48 hours)
  • Step 4: Decision is made and money is released accordingly

Cancellation Policy

Free cancellation up to 2 hours before scheduled time. After that, 25% cancellation fee applies. No-show customers forfeit 50% of payment.
